RPG Handy Helper

RPG Handy Helper is a website that helps you to manage your RPG campaigns. It gives you a tools for managing your characters, items, and quest. Currently project is work in progress.

This tool is being created by a group of friends from collage, who’s addiction to ttrpg-s and great distances, that prohibited them from playing IRL, coupled with interest in IT pushed them to creating this website.

The spark that lit the fire of creation was our first online session through discord. It gave us an idea; idea that could help us in the future and maybe help other just like us who will experience similar problems.

We create this instrument not only for our selfish needs but also for you! That’s right we want to help other players and dms who want to play ttrpg-s but cant meet with their group because of distances between them, work or other adult life things that collide with our wants.

We value your time, money, and privacy. RPG Handy Helper is being created to save you time, help players keep track of their eq and stats and for dms to save their sanity. And as we know time is money and by saving you time we save you money!

Thank you for supporting us and thank you once again for choosing RPG Handy Helper!
